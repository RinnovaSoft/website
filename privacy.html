Introduction

This app was developed by RinnovaSoft. We do not collect any personal data and fully respect your privacy. Below you’ll find detailed information about how data is handled within the app.

Data Storage

This app does not collect or process any personal data. All game data, settings, and statistics are stored locally on your device using internal storage (AsyncStorage). We have no access to this data, and it is not shared or transmitted to external servers.

Internet Connection

The app works entirely offline and does not use network traffic or external connections. You are not required to create an account, and no tracking mechanisms are used.

Voice-Over Function

The voice-over feature uses your device’s built-in text-to-speech functionality. Everything is processed locally: nothing is recorded, transmitted, or stored. The app does not use your microphone.

Questions or Feedback

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this privacy policy, feel free to contact us by email: support@rinnovasoft.nl.
